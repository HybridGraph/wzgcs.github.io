Brief Biography

I'm currently working as a Lecturer in Ocean University of China. Before that, I received my PhD. degree from Northeastern University (China). My research interests include big data analysis and cloud computing, distributed big graph processing and machine learning, and GPU-based parallel computing. I have totally published 19 research papers, some of which are accepted by top conference and journal like ACM SIGMOD and IEEE TPDS and TKDE. I am also maintaining an open-source big graph processing system [HGraph].
